winter sun holidays

In the ruthless winter months, the sun is what one craves to be in. Especially for those living in the colder parts of Europe, visits to locations such as Cape Town, the Caribbean and Egypt where such winter sun holidays can be fully enjoyed. Cape Town with its amazing scenery and beautiful beaches is a must-see for those people wishing to go on cheap beach holidays. The Garden Route and the Overberg Coast are two of the most sought after patches of land with their numerous beaches and restaurants. Overall Cape Town makes the ideal choice for a warm and sunny environment for those who want to escape the cold. Similarly within Africa other destinations offering cheap beach holidays includes Senegal with its wide array beaches and wildlife makes one mesmerized in the beauty of Africa. Senegal also has its own traditional music and culture which makes a visit to this place an enriching experience. Another interesting place to visit on winter sun holidays is Cambodia, with its jungle temples such as those in Angkor and the rural life of Phnom Penh. For cheap beach holidays, Cambodia offers a number of resorts located at its coasts allowing one to enjoy ample sunshine

Indonesia is yet another country located in Asia which is ideal for cheap beach holidays; especially those people take up during the winter months. Goa which is coastal location in India is yet another destination praised for its stunning beaches and can offer one access to cheap beach holidays. In Australia there are a number of ventures where one can go on winter sun holidays such as the beaches of New South Wales with its clear blue water and white sandy beaches. One such beach is known as Bronte beach and even has a small play area for children and for sea bathing purposes. The One Mile Beach at Forster and the Dolphin Bay at the Innes National Park are large strips of beaches which are less crowded than most and thus allow one to enjoy winter sun holidays in peace. In the Caribbean, the Little Corn Island is coastal setting with beaches offering many activities from scuba diving to snorkeling and even a number of reggae dance clubs. Japan’s Iriomote Island is another favorite with beach lovers with its picturesque beaches and canoeing activities is ideal for those going on beach holidays with their loved ones.
