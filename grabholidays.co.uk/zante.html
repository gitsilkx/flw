zante holidays

Zante which is traditionally known as Zakynthos is one of the islands associated with Greece and is located on the Ionian Sea. Being discovered in the Neolithic era, this part of Greece has a rich culture and history, therefore those making holidays to zante who are fond of history will find it to be a paradise of sorts. Some of its most prominent historical sites include the Statue of Dionysius Solomus, the Fanoromeni church, the Cultural center at Dionysius Solomus as well as the Cathedral of Saint Dionysius. The Bochali village located near the capital of Zante i.e. Zante Town, is home to an ancient castle of the Venetian era. Zante’s architecture is predominantly Byzantine and is characterized by its famous landmarks and monuments.

Zante is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Greece according to the past few years which reflect upon how its beautiful island setting attracts a large pool of tourists craving to stay in the sun. Tsilivi is one of its beach like areas which lovely scenery, white sand dunes and clear waters. There are many rural areas with taverns, bars and markets along with one of the most exquisite beaches in the world, located in Laganas. The Smuggler’s Cove at Laganas has a number of turtles residing there which is thus a sanctuary for this endangered species. This beach of white pebbles is only accessible by boat which makes it even more adventurous to go on holidays to Zante. Thus Zante’s shore, both on the east and the west are perfect for family trips to this area, packed with enjoyable activities and a picturesque background similar to those found on postcards. The National Marine Park located at the Bay of Laganas is also a wondrous site. There are a number of scuba diving ventures which allow one to explore the great underwater marine variety present in Zante, such as different species of octopus and seals. Zante also has a number of carnivals and festivals arranged annually such as the jazz festival organized during the summer. There are also a number of bars and clubs that play music of various genres and eras along with the traditional music of this region. The Cape Marathia allows an insight into Greek rural life allowing one a detachment from the rush and fret of the urban lives we are accustomed to by allowing one to indulge in such an exotic experience.
